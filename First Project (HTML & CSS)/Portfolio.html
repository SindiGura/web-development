What to do, what to do...

Stargazing is natural, even if it proves difficult while studying in downtown Toronto. 
There is one star that always stands out, and its not even a star at all. The planet Venus is the first morning and evening star so watch out for it!
Venus and Aphrodite, Greek Origins 
 
Venus, or Ahprodite in greek mythology, was born from the white foam produced by the severed 
Uranus, the personification of heaven, after his son Cronus threw him into the sea. 
Hence, the goddess's name comes from the Greek word aphros, meaning “foam.”
Venus and Aphrodite, Albanian Origins 
 
In Albanian, the word “Aphrodite” or “Aferdita” can be broken into ‘Afer’ (close) and ‘dita’ 
(day). Thus, a literal translation of ‘Close to day’, representing its role as the morning star.
